Troll Games is an indie games developer but make no mistake. Our games don't fall short of a triple A game.

Gogo Manev is our lead developer. He has made games since he was 1 year old and hasn't stopped. He was the genius mind behind such classics as Watch dogs and clicker. Gogo is the one who managed to make the clicker game just a single bite. He has no intention of stopping anytime soon!
Samuil Sharkov is the animator. His talent exceeds everyone's expectations. He once made an animation so smooth that the computer exploded trying to process it. It's thanks to Samuil that Troll Games have such a beutiful scenery for the players to explore.
Teodor Kamchev is the one responsible for our music. It gives a special flare to our games that just pulls you in the world. Once a person was reported missing because he played one of our games. Later it was found out that the music was a bit like that of the pied piper and lured people. We promise that won't happen again!
