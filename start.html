What is TrollGames?

TrollGames is a game company, based in Sofia, Bulgaria. It was found in 2019 by three students. After the release of our first game - Talking Samuil, the company quickly grew up. We currently have over 100 employees working for 6 games, which have over 200 million downloads across all platforms.

What games do you offer?

Our games include Talking Samuil, Humans vs Robots, Watch Dogs and others. They are available on all platforms, you can game on.
